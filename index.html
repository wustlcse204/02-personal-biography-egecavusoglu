Cesme, Izmir Experience

Cesme is a small vacation spot outside Izmir, a city populated over 4 million. The drive takes about an hour with a fairly easy road. There are tens of small coves in Cesme that people go with their cars and spend hours chatting. The water has a deep blue color and it's clear. There are no dangerous animals other than sea urchins. In the evenings me and my friends like many other people would go to Alacati, an antique neighbourhood to hang out in the bars and grab food. Home barbeques, on the other hand are just as good accompanied by hours of deep conversations.
